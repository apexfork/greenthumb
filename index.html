What is the Green Thumb's Mission

The Green Thumbs mission is to help you become an gardener. This app will improve your planting and show you exactly what your plant needs based on your soil PH levels and Moisture levels. We will help you become an expert gardener and teach you the most efficient tricks to make gardening easier! Find the plant you want to help grow

  • Tomatoes When growing Tomatoes the ideal pH is between 6.2 & 6.8. The best temperature would be 68 & 77 degrees, so the spring would be the best time to plant them. When it comes to moisture levels you want between 2.5 & 5.5. Choose this option to find if your soil is suitable to grow tomatoes.
  • Cucumbers When growing Cucumbers the ideal pH is between 6.0 & 6.8. The best temperature needs to be between 75 & 85 degrees, so try growing them in the spring. Moister levels need to between 3.2 & 6.2. Choose this option to find if your soil is suitable to grow Cucumbers.
  • ImpatiensWhen growing Impatiens the ideal pH is between 6.0 & 6.5. For temperature you want it to be between 68 & 85 degrees, so try growing them in the spring. When it comes to Watering them you want to use 0.8 cups of water every 9 days. Choose this option to find if your soil is suitable to grow Impatiens.
